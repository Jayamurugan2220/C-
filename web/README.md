# 🚀 Space Battle Game - Web Version

## 🌐 Online Space Battle Game

A fully functional web version of the Space Battle Game that runs directly in your browser using HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.

## 🎮 Game Features

### **Core Gameplay**
- **3-Level Progressive System** with increasing difficulty
- **Dynamic Health Scaling**: 100 HP → 200 HP → 500 HP
- **Multiple Enemy Types**: Basic, Fast, and Heavy enemies
- **Real-time Combat**: Player vs enemy shooting mechanics
- **Score System**: Points for destroying enemies
- **Level Progression**: Automatic advancement based on score targets

### **Level Configuration**
| Level | Target Score | Player Health | Enemy Count | Enemy Speed |
|-------|-------------|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Level 1** | 300 points | 100 HP | 5 enemies | Normal |
| **Level 2** | 700 points | 200 HP | 8 enemies | 1.5x speed |
| **Level 3** | 1200 points | 500 HP | 12 enemies | 2x speed |

## 🎯 How to Play

### **Desktop Controls**
- **Arrow Keys**: Move your spaceship
- **Space**: Shoot bullets
- **Ctrl**: Alternative shoot button

### **Mobile Controls**
- **Touch Buttons**: On-screen directional pad
- **Fire Button**: Red fire button for shooting
- **Responsive Design**: Works on all mobile devices

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Open the Game**: Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Click "START GAME"**: Begin your space adventure
3. **Destroy Enemies**: Shoot enemies to earn points
4. **Progress Levels**: Reach target scores to advance
5. **Survive**: Avoid enemy bullets and collisions

## 🌟 Game Mechanics

### **Player Ship**
- **Blue Rectangle**: Your spaceship
- **Movement**: 8-directional movement
- **Shooting**: Rapid-fire bullets
- **Health**: Takes damage from collisions and enemy bullets

### **Enemy Types**
- **🔴 Basic Enemies** (Red): Standard speed and health
- **🟡 Fast Enemies** (Yellow): 2x speed, lower health
- **🟣 Heavy Enemies** (Purple): Slow but high health, larger size

### **Combat System**
- **Player Bullets**: Cyan colored, upward trajectory
- **Enemy Bullets**: Red colored, downward trajectory
- **Collision Detection**: Pixel-perfect hit detection
- **Damage System**: Different damage values for different interactions
